Blockchain-Based Pork

Meat safety assurance and traceability is a major challenge in the pork industry. For example, most current systems of product quality inspection rely on 3rd party supervision and still employ random inspection methods, with information poorly organized and vulnerable to manipulation. With features of distributed storage and immutability, the blockchain technology has broad application prospects in terms of building trust for the field of food traceability. Moreover, blockchain-based intelligent contracts allow for rapid real-time whole supply chain-level alarming and subsequent actions, whenever a food safety issue arises. However, the existing agricultural and food blockchain projects rely on external labels, thus bearing the risk of falsification by label swapping. To solve this problem, we are developing a blockchain system coupled with multi-omics data that benchmark the authenticity and quality of pork products. The IOT node throughout the blockchain would further provide rich information for improvement in pig breed.
