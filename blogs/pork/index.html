Blockchain-based pork tracing

Meat safety assurance and traceability is a major challenge in pork industry. For example, most current systems of product quality inspection relies on 3rd party supervision and still adopts random inspection methods, with information poorly organized and vulnerable to manipulation. Featured with distributed storage and immutability, the blockchain technology has shown broad application prospects in building trust for the field of food traceability. Moreover, blockchain-based intelligent contracts allow for rapid real-time whole supply chain-level alarming and subsequent actions, whenever a food safety issue arises. However, the existing agricultural and food blockchain projects rely on external labels, thus bearing the risk of falsification by label swapping. To solve this problem, we are developing a blockchain system coupled with multi-omics data that benchmark the authenticity and quality of pork products. The IOT node throughout the blockchain would further provide rich information for pig breed improvement.
